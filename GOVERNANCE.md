# Governance

CAP is maintained as an open protocol with an explicit separation between:

- the CAP specification
- example implementations
- implementation-specific extensions

Near-term governance goals are to:

- preserve a clear editorial boundary between normative and informative content
- require at least one independent implementation path before new normative surfaces are stabilized
- publish all versioned specification changes in `specification/changelog.md`

Formal maintainership and decision-making processes may evolve over time, but the separation between CAP core, examples, and implementation-specific extensions should remain explicit.
